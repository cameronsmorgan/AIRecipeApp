About ChefGPT

A journey of creativity, flavour, and technology.

The Beginning

It is around a meal that our stories are told and our histories are learnt. Identities are moulded and knowledge is shared. Just as each culture as it's unique history, so too do they have their unique recipes. ChefGPT came about as a means to make learning these recipes modern and seamless process

Process

Powered by Google's Gemini API, ChefGPT takes your ingredients and your selected cusine and language and provides you with personalised recipes. Think of it as Grandma's cookbook in an app

Favourites

Love a recipe? Add it as a favourite! You'll be able to open it up anytime and view it with the same ingredients and process. No need to input ingredients either, it's stored on your device!

Give it a go!

Add your ingredients. Select your cuisine. Select your preferred language and Enjoy!
